# Project Brief – Background Music Website

## Goal
Create a clean, professional website for a background music group that generates event inquiries (weddings, corporate events, private parties).

The site should:
- Feel high-end and trustworthy
- Showcase music through photos and video
- Make it easy to inquire/book
- Load fast and work well on mobile

---

## Target Audience
- Couples planning weddings
- Event planners
- Corporate clients
- Private party hosts

These users care about:
- Professionalism
- Reliability
- Taste / vibe
- Ease of booking

---

## Value Proposition
We provide tasteful, high-quality live background music that enhances events without overwhelming them.

---

## Tone
- Elegant
- Warm
- Professional
- Understated (not flashy or cheesy)

---

## Tech Constraints
- Static site (no backend required)
- Hosted on AWS (Amplify or S3 + CloudFront)
- Media-heavy (photos + video)
- Contact form via third-party (e.g. Formspree)

---

## Success Criteria
- Users understand what we do within 5 seconds
- Users can easily view/listen to examples
- Users can quickly submit an inquiry
