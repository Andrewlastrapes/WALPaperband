# Design Direction

## Overall Style
The site should feel closer to:
- A boutique hotel
- A high-end wedding venue
- A jazz club brand

NOT:
- A loud band promo page
- A generic template site
- A flashy startup landing page

---

## Visual Principles

### 1. Photo-First
- Large, high-quality images
- Minimal text overlays
- Let visuals carry the experience

### 2. Restraint
- Limit colors
- Limit fonts
- Avoid unnecessary animations

### 3. Spacing
- Generous whitespace
- Clean section separation
- No cramped layouts

---

## Typography

### Headings
- Serif or elegant display font
- Example vibe: editorial / refined

### Body
- Clean sans-serif
- Highly readable

### Rules
- Max 2 font families
- Strong hierarchy (clear sizes and weights)

---

## Color Palette

Primary:
- Black / near-black
- Off-white / cream

Accent:
- Muted tone (e.g. deep gold, burgundy, or soft blue)

Rules:
- No bright/neon colors
- Keep contrast high

---

## Layout Structure

- Full-width sections
- Centered content blocks
- Max width ~1100–1200px

---

## Components Style

Buttons:
- Simple
- Solid or outlined
- No heavy gradients

Images:
- Edge-to-edge where possible
- Consistent aspect ratios

Cards:
- Minimal borders or shadows
- Clean and flat

---

## Motion

- Minimal
- Subtle hover states only
- Avoid heavy animations

---

## Inspiration Keywords

- Editorial
- Cinematic
- Intimate
- Timeless
- Minimal
